Your Material Body is a Gift

A programmed video work by Silja Komulainen for Automation, Representation, Transmission at the Leuphana University & Hamburg Media School, Winter Semester 16/17 with lecturer Jonathan Reus.

About this project

In the 60’s, Joseph Weizenbaum created a computer program a human could converse with. The program, ELIZA, acted on a rudimentary set of pre-given rules. Weizenbaum was “startled to see how quickly and how very deeply people (…) became emotionally involved with the computer and how unequivocally they anthropomorphised it”.

Here are two love stories. The stories deal with the computational, but also with the corporeal. The image narrative is partly programmed and random. There are blanks, to be filled with your memories and experiences, imitating the discussions with ELIZA.
